## Features
 * Google Gemini AI — Summarise Videos, Customisable prompts and models
 * Video and Shorts Downloader
 * In-built Video and Audio Muxer
 * Thumbnails and Captions Downloader
 * Ads Blocker
 * Picture in Picture Mode
 * Gesture controls for Volume and Brightness
 * Shows Number of Dislikes
 * Background Audio Player
 * Save videos without logging in (Heart feature)
 * Skip Sponsors (SponsorBlock)
 * Up to 10x video speed
 * Hide Shorts
 * Minimal APK size
